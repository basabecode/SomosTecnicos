### Prompt Hero Loop — Versión A (Ambiental + Equipos)

> **Duración extendida:** 10–12 segundos con dos shots que hacen loop seamless juntos.
> Generar cada shot por separado y unirlos en edición con transición de fundido suave (0.5s).

```
SHOT 1 — Exterior (0–6s):
Seamless cinematic segment. A luxurious modern Colombian home exterior at blue hour — architectural
lighting illuminates clean white walls, large floor-to-ceiling windows glow with warm amber interior
light. Slow, imperceptible rightward camera drift (parallax effect, 0.3° per second). Bokeh foreground
elements: blurred tropical leaves. The sky transitions subtly from deep navy to indigo.
Visible on the facade: a dome-type IP security camera (HIKVISION style, white/chrome) mounted on the
upper-right corner of the wall, its infrared LEDs emit a faint red ring glow. The camera housing is
sleek, modern, perfectly integrated into the architecture. No people, no text, no watermarks.
Mood: aspirational, secure, intelligent home. Color palette: deep wine (#4a0418), warm amber, white,
navy blue. Ultra-realistic, cinematic depth of field, 24fps, photorealistic. Shot on ARRI Alexa,
32mm lens, f/2.8.

SHOT 2 — Interior visible through window (6–12s):
Cinematic close-up pull-back through a large panoramic window of the same home. Inside, slightly
blurred but identifiable: a modern Samsung or LG refrigerator with a digital display glowing soft
blue, a front-load washing machine with a green cycle indicator, a modern gas water heater mounted
on the wall with a flame-status LED, and a WiFi router (TP-Link or ASUS style) on a shelf with
its status LEDs pulsing slowly — one white for power, one blue for wireless signal. The appliances
are arranged naturally in a high-end Colombian apartment/home interior: granite counters, clean tile,
warm pendant lighting. The window glass reflects the exterior garden bokeh.
Camera moves almost imperceptibly outward (slow dolly back). No people. No text. No watermarks.
Color grading: warm amber interior contrasting with the cool indigo exterior night sky outside the
window. Cinematic depth of field, 24fps, photorealistic. Shot on ARRI Alexa, 50mm lens, f/2.2.
```

**Nota de integración:** El Shot 1 termina con un leve zoom-in hacia la ventana → el Shot 2 arranca desde esa ventana hacia afuera. El loop cierra cuando el Shot 2 llega al punto inicial del Shot 1 (misma posición de cámara exterior). Mantener motion blur consistente entre cortes.

```

[4.2s – 6.0s] UTILITY / LAUNDRY AREA:

[6.0s – 7.8s] HOME OFFICE / TECH CORNER:

the router LEDs from the office. Everything glows warmly. The home feels alive with technology.

[9.2s – 10.0s] EXIT:
The drone accelerates upward and exits through a skylight or second-floor window. Final frame:
aerial view of the rooftop with the entire house below — all lights glowing from within.
Fade to the brand color (#4a0418 deep wine). Hard cut.

- Photorealistic, hyperdetailed interior rendering. No cartoon or CGI look.
- Lighting: golden hour warm amber interior + cool blue-hour sky visible through windows.
- Motion: constant fluid movement — no stops, no cuts, no jump edits.
- Camera shake: subtle organic drone wobble (not stabilized perfectly — feels real).
- Depth of field: shallow — each featured appliance is in sharp focus as the camera passes,

- Color grade: rich shadows (#4a0418), warm highlights, teal in shadows (cinematic LUT).
- Lens: virtual 16mm wide angle — slight barrel distortion adds to the drone realism.
- No people. No text overlays. No watermarks. No music reference.
- Frame rate: 24fps. Duration: exactly 10.0 seconds.
- Shot reference style: like the interior fly-through scenes in "Her" (2013) or luxury
  real-estate drone tours — but faster and more purposeful.
```

## NOTAS TÉCNICAS

| Campo | Recomendación |
|---|---|
| **Codec de exportación** | H.264 (web) / ProRes 4444 (edición) |
| **Resolución mínima** | 1920×1080 (16:9) / 1080×1920 (9:16) |
| **FPS** | 24fps (cinematográfico) / 30fps (social media) |
| **Audio** | Exportar muted; agregar música post-producción |
| **Loop** | Usar modo "seamless loop" en Runway o Kling para el hero bg |
| **Tamaño web** | Comprimir a <8MB para el hero bg con FFmpeg |
| **Formato web** | `.webm` para Chrome/Firefox, `.mp4` como fallback |

### Comando FFmpeg para optimizar loop del hero

```bash
# Comprimir a WebM VP9 optimizado para web (<5MB)
ffmpeg -i hero_loop_original.mp4 \
  -c:v libvpx-vp9 -crf 35 -b:v 0 \
  -vf "scale=1920:1080" \
  -an -loop 0 \
  hero_loop.webm

# Fallback MP4 H.264
ffmpeg -i hero_loop_original.mp4 \
  -c:v libx264 -crf 28 -preset slow \
  -vf "scale=1920:1080" \
  -an hero_loop.mp4
```
